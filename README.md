# 🧠 Claude-Marketing-Agents-Latam

**Enterprise-Grade AI Marketing Agents & Neuro-Prompting Architectures**

Welcome to the open-source repository maintained by the engineering and strategy team at Agência GC Marketing. 

## 🎯 The Vision
Traditional digital marketing is dead. The era of bloated SDR teams, generic copywriters, and inefficient funnel builders is over. We are replacing the old agency model with autonomous, highly specialized AI agents powered by Anthropic's Claude framework. 

This repository is our initiative to open-source the proprietary frameworks, agent schemas, and neuro-marketing prompt chains that currently process thousands of high-ticket leads in the Latin American market.

## 📦 What We Are Building
Currently in Active Development (Beta phase). We are continuously pushing our internal agency assets to this public repo:

*   **`/prompts`**: Advanced neuro-marketing prompt chains designed for high-ticket conversion and behavioral framing.
*   **`/workflows`**: Multi-agent orchestration schemas and JSON webhooks for seamless CRM integration.
*   **`/agents`**: Deeply calibrated persona-driven AI agents tailored for B2B qualification and B2C direct response.

## ⚠️ Requirements
To fully utilize these architectures, we highly recommend API access to **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** and the extended context window of **Claude Max** (for multi-agent reasoning and massive dataset context).
